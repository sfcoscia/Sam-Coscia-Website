In my senior year of undergrad at Reed College, I wrote my thesis on Extracting Cosmological Parameters from Cosmic Microwave Background Temperature Map Data, under the mentorship of Dr. Alison Crocker, and astrophysicist and professor at Reed. In this process, I recreated the results from the Planck 2018 papers: Planck 2018 results. V-VI, using power spectrum generation code that I created from scratch in Python. As a result, I learned deep fundamentals of cosmological physics, and got familiar with performing analysis on large astronomical data sets.
